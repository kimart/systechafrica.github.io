## Actuarial settings

Actuarial configurations aids in the setting up actuarial factors that are used
in DB and DC lump sum calculations.

Click the links shown in the screenshot below to set fixed factors and annuity
rates.

<img  alt="actuarial links" width="95%" height="auto"  class="center"  src="../media2/schemeM17.png">  


### Fixed Factors

Fixed factors are provided by the scheme actuaries (fixed factors tables) and
aid in the calculation of pension for DB and DC member benefits based on the
retirement age and the gender type.

### Annuity Rates

Annuity rates are provided by the third-party insurance companies (annuity
factors tables) with the help of internal or external actuaries to aid in the
calculation of pension purchase price or annuities purchase.

Click the **Annuity Rates** link to open the Annuity Rates window and set a new
rate as shown below:
